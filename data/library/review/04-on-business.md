### Lesson 12: Trust the Hand That Feeds

**[04-on-business-111]** · page 73 · 103w · _anxious, trusting, waiting, peaceful_

Sky was a Golden Retriever who lived with an elderly woman in a small cottage. Every morning at six, Sky would sit by his bowl in the kitchen and wait. He never paced. He never whined. He never searched the floor for crumbs or tried to open the pantry door. He simply sat and waited. The woman’s routine varied each day. Sometimes she fed him at six-fifteen. Sometimes not until seven. On difficult mornings when her arthritis flared up, it might be even later. But Sky never panicked. He waited with quiet confidence because he knew she would feed him. She always had.
