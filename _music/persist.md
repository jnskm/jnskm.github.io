## Bible Verse
Galatians 6:9-10 (ASV)

And let us not be weary in well-doing: for in due season we shall reap, if we faint not. So then, as we have opportunity, let us work that which is good toward all men, and especially toward them that are of the household of the faith.

## Inspiration

"Let us not become weary in doing good, for at the proper time we will reap a harvest if we do not give up. Therefore, as we have opportunity, let us do good to all people, especially to those who belong to the family of believers." Galatians 6:9-10.

I want to persist. Even when I am weary. Especially when I am weary.

The road is narrow. The road is long. Some days I am tired. Some days I want to stop. Some days I wonder if any of it matters.

But Paul reminds me: the weariness is not the end. There is a harvest coming. I may not see it today. I may not see it tomorrow. But it is coming. At the proper time. So I will not give up.

I will keep doing good. I will keep loving my brothers and sisters. I will keep walking.

This is what I will do: walk the narrow road until the end. For the Lord. For the family of believers. For the harvest.

Until the end.
