My name is Jin Kim. I am a generalist with no focus. If I had to pick one it would be a focus on people, specifically my family, relatives, and close friends. I like to ponder questions like:

* Why did I do that? Why did she do that?
* What should I learn? What should I read?
* What products should I buy? Which brands should I support?
* What movies should I watch? What shows should I binge on Netflix?
* How can I get stronger and more flexible?
* How can I get healthier? What should I do?
* What should I eat? What should I not eat?

I think about stuff almost every normal human being thinks about. Sometimes I go a little deeper, but all in all I'm just a normal human being trying to figure things out.
