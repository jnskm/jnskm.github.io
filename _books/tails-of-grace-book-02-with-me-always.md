## Twelve Lessons We Learned From Dogs About Grace In Hard Places

Some books teach. This one sits beside you.

*With Me Always* shares stories about dogs, their loyalty, their presence, their quiet wisdom and how those simple lessons point to the love of a faithful God. For anyone facing sickness, poverty, fear, or loneliness, this is a companion for the hard days, and a reminder that you are seen, loved, and still being led home.
